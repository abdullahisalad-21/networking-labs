# Wiring Closet and Rack Basics

## Overview
The wiring closet (IDF/MDF) is the physical heart of a network. Understanding its components is essential for IT support.

---

## 1. Common Components
- Patch panels  
- Switches  
- Routers  
- Racks  
- Cable management trays  
- UPS (battery backup)  

---

## 2. Patch Panels
Used to organize Ethernet cables from wall ports.

---

## 3. Switches
Connect devices within the LAN.

---

## 4. Cable Management
- Velcro ties  
- Horizontal/vertical organizers  
- Labeling cables
